About NEET Preparation Platform

Our Mission

To democratize NEET preparation by providing free, high-quality previous year questions with detailed solutions to students across India.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted NEET preparation platform, helping 1 million+ aspirants achieve medical college admissions.

Key Features

  • 10,000+ curated questions from 2016-2024
  • Detailed solutions with explanations
  • Performance analytics to track progress
  • Topic-wise question filtering
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design
  • Completely free with no hidden charges

Development Journey

Inspiration

As a former NEET aspirant myself, I recognized the need for a comprehensive, user-friendly platform to practice previous year questions with detailed explanations.

Development

Built using Python Flask for the backend, Bootstrap 5 for responsive frontend, and Pandas for data processing. The platform was developed over 3 months with continuous improvements.

Community Impact

Since launch, the platform has helped over 5,000 NEET aspirants improve their scores by an average of 15% through targeted practice and performance analysis.
